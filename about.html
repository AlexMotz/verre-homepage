The Story of Verre

We live in your world

Verre’s founders aren’t your average black car company owners. We’re young entrepreneurs who have consulted at major firms like Morgan Stanley, Goldman Sachs, and Credit Suisse, who found opportunity in the problems we encountered in the financial world. Our ability to close deals was in large part due to our relationship management- ensuring the comfort and style of our offices to reflect the nature of our business. While we could manage the office environment to a high degree, the unpredictability of the car services we used left a bad taste in our mouth, and when we saw the massive overhead and nepotism in the black car industry, we knew we could do better.

We Understand Your Values

Efficiency is the lifeblood of Verre- we seek to help others make more money by eliminating layers of fat that hold companies back. Our small team is based in SoHo and is committed to resolving ground transportation confusion with our ultimate focus on the comfort and experience of our passengers. Verre understands that people who make significant deals care about the margins, and want to be able to control the little things. Vertical alignment means bringing incentive structures together for collective benefit- our chauffeurs understand that our limited roster of premium clients is worthy of every available courtesy because they want to be a part of our network.

The Transportation Revolution Didn’t go Far Enough

We’re living in the most disruptive age in our history- but the advances in consumer ground transportation that ride-sharing applications offer weren’t matched in the corporate space. Companies which demand the most from top-level talent go the extra mile to keep their executives and employees a luxurious workplace experience. They shouldn’t have to break the bank to do so. High level firms which offer executive transportation aren’t served by existing ride-sharing apps because everyday drivers working for volume fares can never replace the experience and patience of a professional chauffeur. Ride-sharing apps are designed for the widest consumer demographic- and their “luxury” offerings are meager and can’t scale.

Why We Verre

Our mission is to provide next-gen business transportation. We relieve corporate employees of stress by offering them an app they can finally rely on, a driver they can speak to, and a price which puts money back into your companies pocket. We seek to transform the experience of travel and work, to marry opulence, efficiency, and relaxation. We Verre because companies shouldn’t settle for drivers which transport anybody with an app- hardworking employees deserve trained chauffeurs supported by the smartest backend in the industry. We Verre because drivers are tired of volume-fare hustle, and want to restore travel to the artful process it once was.
